# Coral Solutions AS

![Coral Solutions Team](/coral_1.gif)

As the former CEO of Coral Solutions AS in Bergen, Norway, I played a pivotal role in revolutionizing design for additive manufacturing. My leadership was instrumental in incorporating the latest optimization algorithms and TPMS/lattice structures, creating a more efficient and eco-friendly design-to-production workflow.

## Highlights of My Tenure

### Advanced Design Optimization

![Design Optimization](/coral_2.gif)

During my time, we significantly enhanced our part redesign process with new AI-driven technologies, achieving unprecedented structural integrity, reduced material waste, and optimized performance and costs.

### Next-Gen Automated Product Design

![Automated Workflow](/coral_3.gif)

I led the refinement of our design automation for industrial and medical parts, significantly improving customization and 3D printing capabilities.

### Innovative Part Consolidation

![Part Consolidation](/coral_5.gif)

### Digitalization & 3D Printing Frontiers
