## How It Works

- **Create unique stories**: Enter a short description of elements like characters, settings, actions, or other ideas.
- **Generate a story**: Click the "Generate Story" button to use AI technology for creating a unique and engaging story.
- **Try your luck**: For those uncertain of what to write, clicking the "Try your luck" button fills the text field with a random sample text for story generation.
